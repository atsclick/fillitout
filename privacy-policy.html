What This Extension Does

Fill It Out helps you fill online forms faster. You type or capture your data once (or upload a document like a resume), and the extension fills matching fields on any web form. Your profile data stays in your browser (IndexedDB / Dexie) and is never shared with anyone without your explicit sign-in and consent.

🔒 Your data is yours

Profile fields, uploaded documents, and form schemas are stored locally first. Cloud sync is optional (Pro feature) and encrypted before leaving your device.
