Fill It Out

Privacy Policy — Version 1.0.1 — Last updated July 2026

🔒 Until you choose to sign in and enable cloud features, nothing leaves your browser. No data is sent to any server. No account needed. No tracking.

What Data Is Collected

Data you provide (stored locally only):

  • Profile fields you type, capture, or upload (name, email, phone, address, etc.)
  • Documents you upload (resume, CV, text files)
  • Form schemas — field layouts of sites you fill (used to improve fills)

Data the extension reads from pages (only on your command):

  • Form field labels, names, and selectors — to match your data to the right field
  • Field values you type — only when you press "Learn" or explicitly ask to capture

When Data Leaves Your Device

Data is never sent anywhere unless you take all of these steps:

  1. Sign in to your account (optional, Pro feature)
  2. Enable cloud features in Dashboard settings:
    • "Cloud sync" — syncs your encrypted profile across devices
    • "Cloud AI" — sends anonymized form structure to our AI provider to help fill difficult questions

Signing in alone does NOT share your data. Your data stays local until you explicitly toggle cloud features on.

If you enable Cloud AI:

  • Form field labels (never your personal values) are sent to our AI provider (OpenRouter / DeepSeek)
  • Uploaded document text may be included to generate better answers
  • We never sell your data. We never train AI on your data.

Data Storage & Encryption

  • Local: All data stored in your browser's IndexedDB — no server involved
  • Cloud (if enabled): Your profile fields are encrypted (AES-256-GCM) before leaving your device. The encryption key is derived from your session — we cannot read your data
  • You can delete all data at any time from Dashboard settings, or by uninstalling the extension

Data Sharing

  • We never sell your personal data
  • We never train AI models on your data
  • Cloud AI uses OpenRouter / DeepSeek — only anonymized field labels, never your values
  • Our backend uses Supabase (PostgreSQL) — see their privacy policy
  • Payments handled by Stripe — see their privacy policy
  • Error logs may include page URLs for debugging — retained 90 days, never shared

Your Choices

  • Use completely offline — never sign in. Nothing leaves your browser
  • Sign in without sharing — signing in does not turn on cloud features
  • Disable cloud AI — toggle off in Dashboard settings
  • Delete your data — "Clear All Local Data" in Dashboard, or delete your account
  • Opt out of analytics — uncheck "Anonymous usage stats" in Dashboard

Data Retention

  • Local data: Stays until you delete it or uninstall
  • Cloud data: Kept until you delete your account
  • Error logs: Retained 90 days
  • Usage stats: Anonymous, aggregated, retained indefinitely
